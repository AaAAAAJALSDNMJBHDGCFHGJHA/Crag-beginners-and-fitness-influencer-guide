Tips and Tricks

  • Warm up before climbing: Get your blood flowing with some light cardio and stretching to prevent injuries.
  • Start with easy routes: Begin with routes that are within your skill level to build confidence and technique.
  • Focus on footwork: Good footwork is essential for efficient climbing. Practice edging, smearing, and hooking.
  • Learn to fall safely: Know how to fall safely to minimize the risk of injury. Practice falling with a partner or instructor.
  • Stay hydrated and fueled: Bring water and snacks to keep your energy levels up during long climbs.
